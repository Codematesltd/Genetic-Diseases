What are Genetic Diseases?

Genetic diseases are disorders caused by changes (mutations) in genes or chromosomes. These conditions can be:

  • Inherited from parents
  • Developed during a person's lifetime
  • Present at birth (congenital)
  • Manifested later in life

Understanding these conditions is crucial for early detection, prevention, and treatment strategies.
